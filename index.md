I'm a founder (Healthengine, GPsite, ADP, Caloriestory, Neospace) who loves helping founders. 

I love the messy, chaotic, stressful, rewarding, intense, and profound experience of being a startup tech founder. I love working with smart people on interesting problems. Adding disproportionate value is very rewarding. Helping people makes me happy. So does winning. 

I usually end up working in these areas:

- Product Development, Willingness to Pay and <a href="/articles/mvp/">Minimum Viable Product</a> (MVP)
- Pricing and Go-To-Market Strategy for digital startups
- AI and Digital Tech
- Capital Raising for Startups
- Founder Mindset and Wellness

FWIW, I have degrees in Law, Accounting and Finance. I've been coding since I was 6.

I live in Perth, Western Australia.

If you're a Perth-based digital founder and want to talk about your startup, <a href="mailto:hello@adamyap.com">drop me a line</a>.

## Toolkit or Stack

These are the tools I use to help me be productive:

### Physical Tools

- Macbook Air
- iPhone
- Pen and Paper
- Whiteboards

### Mental Tools

- The Rule of Threes
- Zen Meditation and Mindfulness

### Meetings

- Phone Calls instead of Meetings
- Walking Meetings
- Very Short Meetings
- Meeting-Free Days

### Project Management

- Standups and Weekly Check-ins
- Time Bound Work instead of Scope Bound Work
- Not Multi-tasking
- Periodic Todo Lists (Daily, Weekly and Monthly)

### Software

- [Markdown](https://daringfireball.net/projects/markdown/) and [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/)
- [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/) and [Claude](https://claude.ai/)
- [Cursor](https://cursor.sh/) and [Claude Code](https://www.claude.com/product/claude-code)
- <span class="mono">[ZSH](https://www.zsh.org/)</span>
- [Laravel](https://laravel.com/)
